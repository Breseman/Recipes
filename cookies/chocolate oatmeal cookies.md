# Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies

## Ingredients
* 2 c sugar
* 2 T cocoa
* ½ stick margarine
* ½ c milk
* ½ c peanut butter
* 3 c quick cooking oats
* 1 t vanilla

## Directions
Mix together and cook on stove 2 mins, then stir in peanut butter, oats, and vanilla. Drop on greased waxed paper. Eat in 10-15 mins.
