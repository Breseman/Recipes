# Beef Stew

## Ingredients
* 2 lbs stew meat
* 1 ½ cloves garlic
* 1 large chopped onion
* 1 c bouillon
* 1 c canned tomato sauce
* Peppercorns
* ¼ c parsley
* 1/3 bay leaf
* 6 potatoes
* 6 carrots
* 1 celery

## Directions
In a large pot, combine meat, garlic, onion, bouillon, tomato sauce, peppercorns, parsley, and bay leaf.

Cook 1 hour.

In the last 15 minutes, add potatoes, carrots, and celery.
