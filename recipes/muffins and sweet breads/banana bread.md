# Classic Banana Bread
350° | 70mins baking

## Ingredients
* ½ c soft butter
* 1 c sugar
* 2 eggs
* 1 1/3 c mashed bananas (3-4 ripe)
* 1 t milk
* 1 t vanilla
* 2 c flour
* 1 t baking soda
* ½ c chopped nuts

## Directions
Cream together butter and sugar.

Beat in eggs, mashed bananas, milk, and vanilla.

Add flour and baking soda.

Stir in nuts.

Bake in greased loaf pan (9x5x3) 350 1hr10min cool
