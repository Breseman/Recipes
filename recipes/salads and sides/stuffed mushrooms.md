# Stuffed Mushrooms

## Ingredients
* 8 large mushrooms
* 6 T salad oil
* 1 c soft bread crumbs
* 1/3 c grated parmesan
* ¼-½ t salt
* ½ t onion powder
* ¼ t Italian seasoning
* 1 egg, lightly beaten

## Directions
Rinse mushrooms, pat dry, and remove mushroom stems for later use.

Brush outside of caps with 4 T oil. Stuff with remaining ingredients; bake.
