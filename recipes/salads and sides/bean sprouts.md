# Bean Sprout Salad

## Ingredients
* 4 c bean sprouts
* 2½ T soy sauce
* 2 T prepared sesame seed
* 1½ t oil
* 2 green onions
* Speck red pepper
* 1 t salt

## Directions
Wash bean sprouts, cover with water, boil. Cook until tender (a couple of minutes), drain. Rinse with cold water to stop cooking.

Add other ingredients, mix to absorb.
