# Ginger Snaps
350°| 9 mins baking

## Ingredients
* 1½ c margarine
* 2 c sugar
* 2 eggs
* ½ c molasses
* 4 c flour
* 2 t baking soda
* 2 t EACH of cloves, cinnamon, ginger

## Directions
Cream butter, sugar, eggs, and molasses.

Add remaining ingredients (not all the flour at once!)

Chill for 2 hrs, roll into small balls

Bake: 350 9 minutes.
