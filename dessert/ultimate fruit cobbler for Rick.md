# Rick’s Ultimate Fruit Cobbler

## Ingredients
### Filling
* 10 c cut up fruit
* ¾ c sugar
* ½ c flour
* ½ t cinnamon
* ½ c lemon

### Crust
* 3 c oatmeal
* ¾ c brown sugar
* ½ t cinnamon
* ¼ t salt
* ½ c melted margarine
* ½ c chopped pecans

## Directions
Preheat oven to 440.

Mix filling ingredients and put in a 9x14” baking dish.

Mix crust ingredients and spread evenly over fruit mixture.

Bake: 15 mins at 440º; 25 mins at 350º.
