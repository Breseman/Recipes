# Irish Soda Bread
350° | 35-40 mins baking

## Ingredients
* 2 c flour
* ½ t baking soda
* 2 t baking powder
* 1 T sugar
* ½ t salt
* 3 T butter
* 1 c raisins
* 1 c buttermilk

## Directions
Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, sugar, and salt.

Cut in butter.

Add raisins and buttermilk; mix well.

Bake in greased 8” layer cake pan. Cut cross ¼” deep across center before baking.
